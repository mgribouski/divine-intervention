Divine Intervention
a medieval magepunk larp
What Is Divine Intervention?
Divine Intervention is a medieval magepunk LARP (Live Action Role Play) beginning in Fall 2017.

The world of Aukana is full of mundane nations warring, trading, and living. The gods of the world--the Five-- have been seen occasionally walking among the people, performing wonders and then disappearing in a flash again. Other than that, though, Aukana is a simple: people just beginning to get a handle on stable society.

At least until about sixteen years ago, when magic suddenly entered the world.

Bolstered by the fact that every farmhand and lord on the planet could suddenly work miracles, society changed. Wars exploded, new alliances formed, and everyone wondered if this magic was going to last. But it remained, and still does.

Divine Intervention begins when the Five issue a decree-- come to a town in their lands to live with them, and learn.

Will you answer the gods' call?
What is the game's setting like?
Divine Intervention is set in the world of Aukana at a town called Brink. Brink is located in the Sacred Lands, a small territory in the North that few have ever entered, said to be run by The Five themselves. A few months ago, a proclamation began to spread through all the nations of Aukana: The Five were establishing a town within the Sacred Lands, where anyone could come, live, and interact with the Five directly for the first time in history.

Brink had its origins centuries ago as a small encampment for The Chosen Few, who until this proclamation were the only ones allowed to freely enter the Sacred Lands at all.
