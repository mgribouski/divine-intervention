Divine Intervention
a medieval magepunk larp
What Is Divine Intervention?
Divine Intervention is an open invitation medieval magepunk LARP (Live Action Role Play) beginning in Fall 2017. Game will be divided annually into four weekend long events during which player’s will assume the personas of their approved characters in order to roleplay and meet challenges presented by Staff. The first three events each year will primarily focus on the playerbase preparing for an expedition into what is known as the Origin of Life, during the final event of the year, in which many dynamics of regular play will be altered or inverted. These four event seasons, each ending in an expedition into the Origin, will continue along a fixed story arc expected to last between three to five years depending on player action.
What is the game's setting like?
For centuries, the world of Aukana has been full of mundane nations warring, trading, and living. At different points, the Gods of the world --known as the Five-- have been seen walking among the people, performing wonders and then disappearing in a flash again. Aside from that, however, Aukana was a simple place, its people just beginning to get a handle on stable society.

At least until nineteen years ago when, in an event that has become known as the Illumination, The Five granted humanity the gift of magic without warning. In an instant, every farmhand and lord on the planet could work miracles.

Society changed. Wars grew far more terrifying than ever before, new alliances formed, and humanity wondered if this Magic would last. But it remained, and with it the people of Aukana have begun taking the world into their own, now capable, hands and shaping it into one of prosperity for all.

Divine Intervention begins when the Five issue a decree: Come. Live with them. Learn. So that you might ensure the future of Humanity in the same place it began… within the Origin of Life.
